How to Play

Make as many words as you can using the letters shown. You have 10 seconds per round!

The letters will change 5 times, giving you different words to work with.

Scoring:

  • Each word earns points equal to its length
  • Finding the source word multiplies its score by 10
  • After all rounds, create a sentence using your found words
  • Each word from your collection used in the sentence earns 3 bonus points
